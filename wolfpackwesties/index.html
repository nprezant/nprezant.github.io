We are the West Coast Swing Club at NC State! We welcome everyone (of all skill levels) to join us for free lessons and dances one night a week in the Carmichael Rec Center.

Place: Carmichael Rec Center, 2nd Floor, Studio C
Time: Tuesdays 9:00-11:00pm
Attire: Comfortable clothing
